Explore Our Mission

A Platform Built on Three Pillars

Our goals are achieved through a holistic approach focusing on Faith, Community, and Economy.

We leverage technology to make authentic Islamic knowledge more accessible and engaging than ever before.

  • AI-Powered Tafsir: Get instant, context-aware explanations for any Quranic verse.
  • AI Pronunciation Coach: Perfect your Tajweed by recording your recitation and receiving immediate feedback.
  • Local Sabahan Reciters: Immerse yourself in the Quran with recitations from respected Qaris across Sabah.

Our platform serves as a digital bridge, connecting Muslims across Sabah and providing vital community services.

  • Ustaz Radhi AI: Chat with our culturally-aware Islamic AI assistant who speaks Bahasa Malaysia and provides authentic guidance based on Mazhab Syafie teachings, specifically designed for Sabahan Muslims.
  • Community Voice (Suara Komuniti): Report social issues or request aid, ensuring support reaches those who need it most.
  • Mosque & Event Directory: Find your nearest mosque and stay updated on local Islamic events.

A strong ummah is an economically empowered one. Our platform creates tangible opportunities for local Muslims.

  • Halal Marketplace: Discover and purchase products from local Muslim entrepreneurs.
  • Teacher Marketplace: Certified local Asatizah can offer paid, personalized lessons.
  • Digital Zakat & Waqf: Fulfill your religious obligations transparently to support local causes.
